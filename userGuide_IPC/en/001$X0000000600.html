Parts Tab (IPC)

The Parts Tab shows a table of Information relevant to the current area of the vehicle being viewed:

    >
    >
    >
    >
    >

Clicking on any part of a parts line will highlight that part on the 3D Model.

If no 3D model is available, but a 2D image is, then clicking the part line will open the 2D image.

Moving the mouse point over a part line without clicking on it will highlight it in blue.

The part line will change to a sand colour and remain this colour when selected by clicking on it.

Clicking on a Part on the 3D Model will highlight the corresponding part line.

Selecting the small arrow next to each part line will open a small sub table of information relevant to that part. This includes:
